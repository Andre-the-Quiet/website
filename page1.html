Mochi Madness

I was the engineer and co-designer for this game. My partner and I used the Unity engine to create this game over 15 weeks for our Intermediate Game Development course.


I learned so much while developing this game, as it was my first time programming in Unity. I got familiar with Unity's architecture as well as learned how to work under agile development.


It was also particularly educational because halfway into the semester, we had to switch to working remotely due to COVID-19. Despite that, we created a game that was silly, competitive and cute.
