Serpent Showdown

I led a 3-person design team to create a 3D arena fighter, intended to release on the Nintendo Switch. I designed and tune the combat mechanics, including a "reflect" that knocks projectiles back at the opponent. I also communicated with engineering, art, and usability to refine the game's feel and accessibility. I worked with Unity to greybox the arena, test mechanics, and implement features.
