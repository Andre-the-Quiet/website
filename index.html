Engineering Experience

As a student I have re-created many classic arcade games using C++

Super Mario Bros. (1985)

A platformer game released for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES).  You play as Mario and traverse across eight worlds to save Princess Peach from Bowser, the Koopa King

I implemented a scrolling camera that follows Mario only when traveling towards the right side of the screen.  I also handled all the collisions between Mario, Goombas, and the platforms.  

Platform: Visual Studio

Development time: 1 week

The Legend of Zelda (1986)

An action-adventure game released for the NES.  You play as Link, seeking eight fragments of the Triforce so you can save Princess Zelda from the evil Ganondorf.

I used a tile map csv file to create the dungeon and implemented collectibles the player could pick up and use later, such as a key to open a locked door.  I programmed secret blocks the player had to push in a specific direction to unlock certain doors as well as coded all the enemy behaviors.

Platform: Visual Studia

Development time: 1 week

Space Tunnel

This 3D game is not emulating any specific game, but is inspired by games like X-Wing Alliance (1999) where you fly a space ship into a narrow corridor and must avoid obstacles in your path.

I implemented a camera that follows the ship as it moves forward through the corridor using matrix transformations.  I also generate more obstacles and game objects in front of the ship as it continues forward to create an endless mode.

Platform: Visual Studio

Development time: 1 week

Game Design

I also have skills and experience in design, which allows me to communicate better with both designers and engineers.

Modern Solutions

Modern Solutions is a wacky card game where you and your friends play as employees at Bizarre Corp, a company that seeks to solve ANY problem in the universe….even if they’re not qualified for it. You can play as the Boss and come up with wacky problems for the other players to solve using just the prompts on the cards. The other players will play as Agents that will not only need to form a team with their cards, but also convince the Boss that their solution is better than their opponents, even if it means using a little sabotage!

Application to Engineering
