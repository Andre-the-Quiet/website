Game Design

Project "Snek Pit"
I lead a 3-person design team to create a 3D arena fighter using the Nintendo Switch Joy-cons.  I designed and tune the combat mechanics, including a "reflect" that knocks projectiles back at the opponent.  I also communicate with the engineering, art, and usability teams to refine the game's feel and accessibility.  I work in the Unity engine to greybox the arena, test mechanics, and implement features.

Modern Solutions 
Modern Solutions is a wacky card game where you and your friends play as employees at Bizarre Corp, a company that seeks to solve ANY problem in the universe….even if they’re not qualified for it.  I wrote documentation and held playtests during this game's development.  I also helped revise and streamline the game's rules for simplicity.

Application to Engineering

As an engineer, communicating with designers is crucial in game development. So having knowledge of game design lets me more accurately translate a designer's ideas into code.  As a tech designer, I am also able to implement and prototype design concepts that could add to the experience.

Solo Projects

As a student I have re-created many classic arcade games using C++

Super Mario Bros. 

I implemented a 2D parallax camera and collision boxes for both players and enemies.
I handled all the enemy behaviors as well as sounds, animation, and the win/lose states.

Platform: Visual Studio
Development time: 1 week

The Legend of Zelda

I used a tile map csv file to create a 2D dungeon and spawn enemies.
I implemented collectibles, such as a key, that the player could pick up and use later.
I programmed different enemy behaviors, including the boss who has a normal state and a rage state.

Platform: Visual Studio
Development time: 1 week

Space Tunnel

I implemented a 3D camera that follows the ship as it moves forward through the corridor.
I continuously generate more obstacles and game objects in front of the ship as it continues forward to create an endless mode.
I also recursively programmed chain explosions for certain obstacles.

Platform: Visual Studio
Development time: 1 week
