"Wheelin' and Mealin'"

A co-op, online multiplayer, party game where players take on the role of a driver and cook duo aiming to serve delicious food to the denizens of the city. I work with the design team to ideate on the interplay between cooking and driving. Mochi Madness

a competitve, 2-player, 3D action game where you play as stick-wielding mochi trying to make the perfect dango stick. I was a co-designer and the main engineer for this game. I programmed most of the core mechanics of the game, such as the poking & throwing of objects, and the unique effects each interactable object has on the players.

Serpent Showdown

I worked with the design team to create a 3D arena fighter, intended to release on the Nintendo Switch. I designed and tuned the combat mechanics, including a "reflect" that knocks projectiles back at the opponent. I also communicated with engineering, art, and usability to refine the game's feel and accessibility. I worked with Unity to greybox the arena, test mechanics, and implement features.

Space Tunnel

I implemented a 3D follow camera that tails behind the ship as it moves through the corridor.
I continuously generate more obstacles ahead of the ship to create an endless mode.
I recursively programmed chain explosions for the red blocks that destroy neighboring blocks.

Platform: Visual Studio
Development time: 1 week

A Legend of Zelda Dungeon

I used a tile map to create a 2D dungeon and spawn enemies.
I implemented collectibles, such as a key that the player could pick up and use on locked doors.
I programmed the different enemy behaviors, including the boss' normal and rage states.

Platform: Visual Studio
Development time: 1 week
